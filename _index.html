Brief About Me

"I rather think that archives exist to keep things safe – but not secret.” -Kevin Young

My philosophy: To work effectively while processing and creating information swiftly. I enjoy the challenge of learning and adapting within a new work environment. Collaboration and teamwork are an important concept of a job because I thrive in a group environment. Additionally, I work well independently and keep myself on task because of self-pacing. Following setbacks, I return to the start of the project, reassess the situation, compartmentalize tasks, and cautiously begin the process again.

Research Interest

I am interested in history, theatre, music, historic preservation, museums, dance, libraries, archival work, performance, and historical/geneaological research.
