    Projects:

    Loops for Love Project

    • As the secretary of the Knitting and Crochet Club at the University of Delaware, I coordinated with board members such as the philanthropy and media communications coordinators for the Loops for Love project in partnership with Lankenau Medical Center. During this project club members knit and crocheted hearts for ICU patients and their families during the Covid-19 pandemic.
